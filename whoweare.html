Who We Are?

Arka Jain University

Arka Jain University (AJU) is a premier private institution known for fostering academic excellence, innovation, and practical learning. With modern infrastructure and a diverse academic environment, AJU empowers students to become future-ready professionals.

Hack Horizon

Hack Horizon is the flagship hackathon of AJU, designed to bring developers, designers, innovators, and creators together under one roof. The event celebrates problem-solving, creativity, and real-world innovation through competitive challenges and collaboration.

GDG on Campus AJU is a tech community where students explore Google technologies, build impactful solutions, and collaborate on projects. It aims to cultivate technical talent through workshops, study jams, and developer events.

Code & Compute Society

Code & Compute Society (CCS) is AJU's official coding and tech club focused on fostering programming skills, peer learning, and project development. CCS regularly hosts coding contests, workshops, and collaborative learning sessions.
